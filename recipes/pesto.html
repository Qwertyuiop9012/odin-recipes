Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a delicious and classic dish that combines the vibrant flavors of fresh herbs, garlic, nuts, cheese, and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onmion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese
